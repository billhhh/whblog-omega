Personal Introduction: I am currently a research fellow at Australian Institute for Machine Learning (AIML), the University of Adelaide, working with Prof. Gustavo Carneiro. I received my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Chunhua Shen and Dr. Qi Wu. Before that I was honored to have worked with Prof. Steven Hoi at the Singapore Management University on Computer Vision research. My research interests lie in Medical Machine Learning, Multi-modal Learning, Computer Vision, Security of AI Models, Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning Theory, etc.
