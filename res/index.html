Personal Introduction: I am currently a research fellow at Australian Institute for Machine Learning (AIML), the University of Adelaide, working with Prof. Gustavo Carneiro. I received my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of Prof. Chunhua Shen and Dr. Qi Wu. Before that I was honored to have worked with Prof. Steven Hoi at the Singapore Management University on Computer Vision researches. My research interests lay on Multimodal Learning, Computer Vision, Medical Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning Theory, etc.
