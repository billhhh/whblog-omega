Introduction: I am currently a research fellow at Australian Institute for Machine Learning (AIML), the University of Adelaide, under the supervision of Prof. Gustavo Carneiro, Dr. Lingqiao Liu and Dr. Qi Wu. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student supervised by Prof. Chunhua Shen and Dr. Qi Wu. I have submitted my thesis in May 2021. I am doing research across multiple domains: Computer Vision, Multimodal Learning, Medical Image Processing, Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning Theory and Security of AI models. Multiple internationally recognised exceptional records and outstanding achievements have been granted to me. I have published multiple papers on top-tier conferences, including ECCV (Core Ranking A, visual-and-language navigation), IJCAI (Core Ranking A*, unsupervised representation learning), ACM multimedia (Core Ranking A*, fully quantized super-resolution) and MICCAI (Core Ranking A, chest X-Ray medical image processing). Recently, one AI security paper has been accepted by ACISP-2021 and two papers are in submission to IEEE Transactions Journal ACM Computing Survey (Core Ranking A*). Another paper w.r.t. traffic optimization with deep reinforcement learning is submitted to an IEEE Journal. I have several patents, related to robots control and computer vision surveillance, that are authorized as well. Since the beginning of my Ph.D., I am doing a mining research project with OZ Minerals to automate the mill parameters setting. The purpose of this project is to combine data science and traditional mineralogy to further promote the Mining Industry in Australia. In 2016, I have interned in Ant Financial Services Group affiliate with Alibaba Corporation (bank related techs) and got the official job offer. The newest version of my cv is at https://billhhh.github.io/whblog-omega/res.
